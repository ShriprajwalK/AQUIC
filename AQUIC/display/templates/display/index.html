Wireless sensor mesh netowork of nodes

Each node is micro-controller based and has different kinds of sensors like MQ135, MQ4 etcetra. on it. The nodes are dispersed throughout the city. They transmit data to the sink node. The data transmitted back has several uses(ex: Monitoring) and is also used to train a machine learning model that can predict future AQI values..

The sensor nodes detect air pollution by measuring the concentration of various harmful gases and dust particles. This gives out a number known as the Air Quality Index or AQI.
The sensor nodes detect water pollution by measuring the pH, concentration of salts etc. This is very useful information for Fishermen with which they can understand the types of fish and where they can be found.

Land

The sensors on the nodes measure soil pollution by measuring the contamonation in the soil due to harmful substances like Urea, DDT etc. This information is very useful for farmers as they can estimate the fertility of their soil and hence decide on the crops to be planted.
